Tech Idoltary's Development Methodology

To make sure that your project is delivered on time and without any hassles,
we have crafted an all-encompasing framework comprised of 6 major phases:

1. Requirement Gathering and Understanding

Our experts will work with you to identify all your requirements. We will make sure that these are complete and there are no gaps between your expectations and what our designers and developers will create and/or build.

2. Development Estimation

We will share and discuss with you our quotation and development time estimates.

3. Development Kick-Off

With the quotation and time estimation approved, we will introduce our team. No matter how many people are going to be involved in the actual project, we will provide you with a single point of contact (project manager) to ease the communication process.

4. Concept

Our designers and/or developers will submit the first draft for your approval. We will revise the concept based on your feedback to verify that the proposed solution meets all your unique requirements.

5. Delivery

Our project manager will make sure that the development of your project is always on track! He/She will proactively update you on the progress and will be happy to discuss any concerns you might have.

6. Maintenance

Tech Idolatry's Developers know that projects especially websites don't stop when the final product is delivered! Our expects will work to keep your website up and running so you can focus on your business and personal goals.
